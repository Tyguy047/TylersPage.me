Who am i?

Tyguy47.com

One of the first websites that I created and published was Tyguy47.COM
I made this site with Google sites and this is also when I bought my first domain. I still try to maintain this site to this day even thought it was my first website. I think that Google sites is an amazing tool and I would recommend it for both beginners and professionals!
However one of the reasons that I decided to make my own site was because of the non-removable watermark that Google places in the bottom left of the site. I think that it is important to be able to display your work and content without being watched over by larger companies and being in fear that they may take your site offline. This is why someday I would like to be able not only develop my own websites but also host them!
